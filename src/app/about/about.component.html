Education – Door to The Future

Education's purpose is to replace an empty mind with an open one. No child should be left behind. 95% of Indian children attend primary school but barely 55% of them end up reaching higher secondary school. The most recent ASER report also shows that learning levels in Government- run primary schools are alarmingly low. This implies that almost half the children drop out after 8 years of elementary education, without acquiring minimum acceptable foundational skills.

Elderly - Backbone of family

People live with dignity all their life and suddenly one day they are left with no choice but to beg to survive. In villages, many elderly are left alone after their children move to the cities in search of better livelihood. In last few days of their lives, who will they look up to? Please do not give up on them easily. You are present only because of your parents. Be thankful to them and hold them tight.

Mental Health - To prevent emotional breakdown

Physical and financial health are important but not as much as compared to one's Mental health. An effect on mental health can cause an emotional breakdown leading to many serious complications. 1 in 4 people always get have issues regarding their mental health. We are taking an active role in reducing the stigma around mental illness and expanding access to mental health care.
